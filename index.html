Bio

Born and raised in the greater Seattle area I have only lived in the state of Washington. I first got a taste of programing in my early teens when I self taught HTML in order to make my my-space look 'cool' and load quickly. In high school I took a class in network programming through Cisco, though interesting, I didn't love it. Outside of a few computer science classes in college it wasnt until seven years later that I decided to give web developement a shot, remembering how much I enjoyed messing with HTML. Even though almost everything had changed other than the basics of HTML the process of self teaching made it clear that it was something I could really enjoy. In an effort to learn more than I could get be teaching my self and to get connections to the buisness world I found Code Fellows. The five months there I learned most of what I know now.
